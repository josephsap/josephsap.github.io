Prototype: Seventh Generation Baby Photobomb

While at Made, I prototyped the initial functionality of a project called Baby Photobomb for client Seventh Generation. My task was to create the photo cropping placement functionality and output the final coordinates which would be used later to generate the final image. I made a clipping mask with a photo behind it, and if the image was placed out of the crop, the prototype would automatically bump it back in place.

Have a look at the console to see the final coordinate output.
